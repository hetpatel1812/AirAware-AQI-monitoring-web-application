About AirAware

India's most comprehensive real-time air quality monitoring platform

🎯

Our Mission

AirAware is dedicated to making air quality information accessible, understandable, and actionable for every Indian citizen. We believe that awareness is the first step toward cleaner air, and our platform empowers individuals, communities, and policymakers with real-time pollution data.

📊

What We Do

We monitor and report the Air Quality Index (AQI) across hundreds of cities in India, covering all major states and union territories. Our platform tracks key pollutants including PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, CO, and O₃, providing a comprehensive picture of the air you breathe.

🔬

Our Methodology

We use the Indian National Air Quality Index (AQI-IN) standard established by the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB). This standard classifies air quality into six categories ranging from "Good" (0–50) to "Severe" (401–500), helping you quickly understand the health implications.

🌍

Why It Matters

India faces significant air quality challenges, particularly during winter months. With over 1.4 billion people, access to reliable AQI data is critical for public health. Our platform helps you make informed decisions about outdoor activities, travel, and protective measures.
